Your Privacy Matters

At Pelican Gate, we are committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This policy explains clearly what data we collect, why we collect it, and how it is handled.

We will never sell, rent or share your personal data with third parties for marketing or commercial purposes.

Information We Collect

When you submit a ticket request through our website, we collect the information you provide: your name, email address, phone number, country of residence, and the details of the event you are looking for, including preferred seating, budget and any additional comments.

We collect only what is necessary to process your request and respond to you effectively.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively to review your ticket request, search for available options and contact you with relevant results. We do not use your data for any purpose beyond the service you have requested.

Data Security

We apply appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, loss or misuse. Our website operates over secure HTTPS connections at all times.

Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as is necessary to fulfill the purpose for which it was collected, or as required by applicable law. You may request the deletion of your data at any time by contacting us directly.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct or request deletion of any personal data we hold about you. To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at contact@pelicangate.com and we will respond promptly.
