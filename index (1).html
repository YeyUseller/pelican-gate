Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

1. Who We Are

Pelican Gate LLC operates pelicangate.com, a personalized ticket sourcing service. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use and protect your personal data.

2. Data We Collect

When you submit a ticket request through our website, we collect:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number / WhatsApp
  • Country of residence
  • Event details (name, city, venue, date)
  • Number of tickets and seating preferences
  • Approximate budget
  • Additional comments you provide

3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to respond to your ticket request, communicate available options and provide our ticket sourcing service. We do not sell, share or transfer your personal data to third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Data Retention

We retain your request data for as long as necessary to complete the service or as required by applicable law. You may request deletion of your data by contacting us at contact@pelicangate.com.

5. Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, loss or misuse.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at contact@pelicangate.com.
